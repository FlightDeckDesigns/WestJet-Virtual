About Us

WestJet Virtual is a premier virtual airline dedicated to providing an immersive and realistic flight simulation experience. We operate primarily on the Infinite Flight platform, offering pilots a chance to fly a variety of routes inspired by the real-world operations of WestJet Airlines.

Our Fleet

Our diverse fleet mirrors that of WestJet Airlines, including aircraft such as the Boeing 737 series and the Bombardier Q400.

Routes

We offer a wide range of domestic and international routes, connecting major cities across North America and beyond.

Our Staff

  • Chief Executive Officer (CEO): Oversees all operations and strategic planning.
  • Chief Operating Officer (COO): Manages daily operations and ensures departmental coordination.
  • Flight Operations Manager: Supervises pilot schedules and flight procedures.
  • Human Resources Manager: Handles pilot recruitment and internal communications.
  • Events Director: Organizes and promotes virtual events.
  • Training Director: Oversees pilot training programs.
  • Media & Design Manager: Manages branding and promotional materials.
  • Chief Technology Officer (CTO): Maintains the website and technical systems.
